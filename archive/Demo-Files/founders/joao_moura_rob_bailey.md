# João Moura & Rob Bailey — CrewAI

---

## Background

João Moura is the primary technical founder and creator of CrewAI, the open-source multi-agent AI framework. He is a Brazilian-born serial entrepreneur with an engineering background who identified the need for a structured, role-based approach to multi-agent AI orchestration. Rob Bailey co-founded CrewAI alongside Moura, contributing business and engineering expertise. Together they secured $18M in Series A funding from Insight Partners in October 2024.
