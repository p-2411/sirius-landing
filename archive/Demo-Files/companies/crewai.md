## What They Do

CrewAI is an open-source framework for orchestrating networks of role-playing autonomous AI agents. It enables developers to define agents with specific roles, goals, and tools, then coordinate them to complete complex multi-step tasks collaboratively — similar to how a human team operates. CrewAI has become one of the most popular agent orchestration frameworks in the open-source ecosystem.

---

## Why It Matters

The shift from single LLM calls to coordinated multi-agent systems is one of the most significant architectural trends in AI. CrewAI provides a structured, intuitive framework for building these systems, dramatically lowering the barrier to deploying production-grade agentic workflows. With 150K+ GitHub stars and 150+ enterprise customers, the market has clearly validated the approach. The $18M Series A from Insight Partners confirms institutional confidence.
