# Activepieces

**Overall Conviction Score: 7.4**

---

## What They Do

Activepieces is an open-source, AI-first no-code workflow automation platform that enables businesses and developers to connect over 330+ applications and automate complex workflows without writing code. It serves as a self-hostable alternative to Zapier and Make, with a strong emphasis on AI-native integrations and enterprise deployments.

---

## Why It Matters

Automation is the backbone of operational efficiency at every company. As AI adoption accelerates, teams need infrastructure to connect AI models with real business tools — CRMs, databases, communication platforms, and more. Activepieces sits at the intersection of AI and automation, offering an open-source foundation with a growing enterprise layer. With 100K+ active installations and $1.7M ARR already achieved on minimal funding, the product-market fit signals are strong.
