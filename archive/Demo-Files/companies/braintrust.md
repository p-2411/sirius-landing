# Braintrust

**Overall Conviction Score: 6.6**

---

## What They Do

Braintrust is an AI observability and evaluation platform that helps engineering teams build, test, and ship high-quality production AI products. It provides tools for evaluating LLM outputs, logging and tracing AI calls, A/B testing prompts, and monitoring production AI systems — creating a complete quality assurance layer for AI-powered applications.

---

## Why It Matters

As AI moves into production, quality and reliability become existential concerns. Braintrust addresses the critical gap between rapid AI prototyping and production-grade deployment. The platform's evaluation-first approach — helping teams measure what good looks like before shipping — is increasingly essential as AI errors can have real business consequences. The a16z backing and $800M valuation at Series B validate the size of this opportunity.
