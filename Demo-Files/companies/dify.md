# Dify

**Overall Conviction Score: 6.6**

---

## What They Do

Dify is an open-source platform for building, deploying, and operating production-grade agentic AI workflows. It provides a visual workflow builder, prompt engineering tools, RAG pipeline management, agent orchestration, and model management — all in a single integrated environment. Dify sits between low-code AI builders (like Bubble for AI) and full code frameworks (like LangChain), giving teams a powerful yet accessible way to ship AI applications.

---

## Why It Matters

The gap between AI experimentation and production deployment is enormous. Dify fills this gap by providing a complete platform — visual workflow construction, LLM prompt management, agent orchestration, and production monitoring — that dramatically reduces time-to-production for AI applications. Its massive global adoption (reflected in the "Massive GitHub stars" signal) and Product Hunt presence demonstrate genuine product-market fit across developer communities worldwide.

---

## Target Customer

- Developers and product teams building AI-powered applications without deep ML expertise
- Enterprise AI teams needing a visual, managed environment for deploying LLM workflows
- Startups and mid-market companies that want RAG pipelines and agent workflows without extensive custom engineering
- Global developer market, particularly strong in Asia-Pacific and emerging markets
