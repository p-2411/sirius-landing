# Weights & Biases
**Conviction Score:** 7.3/10
**Category:** Observability / MLOps
**Website:** https://wandb.ai

## What It Does
Weights & Biases (W&B) is an MLOps platform providing experiment tracking, model monitoring, dataset versioning, and AI observability. It enables ML teams to log, visualize, and compare training runs, manage model artifacts, and monitor deployed models in production — all through a developer-friendly SDK and collaborative dashboard.

## Why It Matters
As AI model development has become central to enterprise product strategy, the complexity of training, evaluating, and deploying models at scale has grown dramatically. W&B solves a critical coordination and visibility problem: without experiment tracking and observability, ML teams lose reproducibility, waste compute, and ship unreliable models. W&B has become the gold standard for ML teams that take model quality seriously.

## Target Customer
- ML engineers and data scientists at companies training custom models
- Enterprise AI/ML teams requiring governance, reproducibility, and compliance across model development
- Research labs and academia building and publishing frontier models
- LLM fine-tuning teams needing evaluation and prompt tracking
