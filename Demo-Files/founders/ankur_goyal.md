# Ankur Goyal — Braintrust

---

## Background

Ankur Goyal is the founder and CEO of Braintrust, an AI observability and evaluation platform backed by a16z. Prior to founding Braintrust, he was a senior infrastructure engineer at Figma, where he worked on scaling the company's core collaborative design infrastructure. Before Figma, he co-founded Impira, a document intelligence startup. His background spans systems engineering, machine learning infrastructure, and product development at high-growth technology companies.
