## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment:** The dominant AI tells are gone. The eyebrow-on-every-section is now a deliberate numbered narrative arc (01–05); pricing is no longer a MOST-POPULAR-pill card grid (Pro is the single framed gold card, Free/Max are quiet columns); the side-stripe is removed; the spacing reads as one intentional rhythm rather than three competing systems. The page now reads as crafted and on-brand — a confident editorial-product hybrid, not a template.

**Deterministic scan:** **0 findings** (was 1). Clean.

**Robustness:** content no longer ships blank to non-scrolling renderers — the prerendered HTML went from 7 content-hiding `opacity:0` to 0; reveals are transform-only with opacity pinned at 1.

**Contrast:** muted text now clears WCAG AA — `ink-4` measured ~4.96:1 (was ~2.7), `ink-3` 5.9–6.75:1.

## What's Working

1. **The hero still carries the page** — Fraunces display, gold italic accent line, the cyan orb, dark restraint. Untouched and still the strongest asset.
2. **The narrative arc reads as intent, not scaffolding.** 01→05 gold ordinals turn what were generic kickers into a "day with your cofounder" spine; the band-deep backgrounds carry the sectioning so the unified spacing never feels monotone.
3. **Outcome voice, now sharper.** The §01 rewrite names the real friction (keeping context current) and the connect-once wedge — the clearest articulation of the value on the page.

## Remaining Issues (honest)

- **[P2] Signature canvas motion vs reduced-motion.** The reveals now honor `prefers-reduced-motion`, but the voice orb and starfield are JS/canvas animations — verify they degrade (pause/static) under reduced-motion. PRODUCT.md keeps motion core *with* reduced-motion fallbacks where motion conveys meaning. **Command:** `/impeccable audit` (motion/a11y).
- **[P3] Sparse right-column visuals.** The WorldGraph and ToolOrbit are airy by design, but next to short copy on the arc sections they read a touch low-density. Acceptable and on-brand; only worth revisiting if a section feels empty. **Command:** `/impeccable layout` (only if desired).
- **[P3] Full-height hero.** The hero is `min-h-screen`, pushing the proof line low on tall viewports. Intentional, but a slightly shorter hero would surface the value faster. Optional.

## Questions to Consider

- Do the orb and starfield need a reduced-motion still-state, or are they gentle enough to keep? (One quick decision closes the last real a11y gap.)
- Is the full-height hero earning its height, or would a tighter hero get the proof in front of skeptics sooner?
